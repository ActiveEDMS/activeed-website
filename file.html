Our Story

I decided to pursue this venture after my son began taking part in toddler football sessions. I was shocked by the lack of care, enthusiasm, and organisation — and felt that paying parents and children deserved so much more. As a Director of Sport with nine years of PE‑teaching experience, I understand the power and importance of movement and motor development. Having taught from Reception through to Post‑16, I bring wide expertise that forms the core of ActiveED Multi‑Sports.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the best parent‑and‑child sporting experience on the market. We are evidence‑informed, passionate, knowledgeable, and caring. We want every child to be excited for their next session and to leave feeling stronger — physically, socially, and mentally.
